Quick and Delicious, Wheat Free Biscuits

Michelle K

Being gluten or wheat free is difficult, one of the main food items my son misses is bread.  Our bread today is just not at all what it used to be and it is lacking nutrients and enzymes that once were there prior to so much processing.  This recipe is a very easy go to for me and I make it almost every day.  My son cannot have wheat or his tummy will hate him for it, so we have found spelt or barley flour to be preferrable for him.  I have tried this recipe with my King Arthur's Gluten Free flour and it did not work for me, but if you're brave go for a gluten free try!

Ingredients

2 cups spelt flour

3 teaspoons baking powder

1/2 teaspon salt 

3/4 cup water

1/4 cup oil (we use olive oil)

Directions

Pour all dry ingedients into a bowl and mix.  Then add wet ingredients and stir until dough is formed.  Spoon 2 tablespoons of dough for each biscuit on a cookie sheet.  Makes 6-9 biscuits.  Bake at 400° for 10-12 minutes. I like to take the measuring cup I had the oil in and mix about 2 teaspoons of honey in it and brush that on top of the biscuits once they come out of the oven.
