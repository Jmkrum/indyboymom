Goldspoon Chili

Michelle K

Odd name for chili perhaps, but it is award winning and the award I won was in fact a gold spoon.  At a friends wedding reception they had multiple people make chili and after the ceremony the bridal party took photos and guests enjoyed eating then voting on their favorite chili.  I won a gift card and a gold glitter sprayed spoon trophy, we had a blast!  Without further ado here is the winning chili recipe!  

  • Ingredients
  • 2 pounds ground beef chuck
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • ½ pound bacon (okay, we usually do 1 pound of bacon and 1/2 pound of sausage!)
  • 3 cans (15 oz) chili beans drained
  • 1 can (15 oz) chili beans in spicy sauce
  • 2 (28 oz) cans diced roasted tomatoes with juice
  • 1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 stalks celery
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 4 cubes beef boullion or we use 2 tablespoons liquid aminos
  • ¼-1/2 cup water
  • ¼ cup chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves pressed
  • 1 tablespoon oregano leaves
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons siracha chili sauce (1 tsp for less spice)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (1/2 tsp for less spice or 1 1/2 for more if you're braver than I)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ½ tablespoon sugar (we use honey or maple syrup)

 

Directions

Brown beef and sausage in large skillet over medium heat. Drain.
Cook bacon in skillet over medium heat.
Combine meats.
Chop peppers, onion and celery then heat over medium-high heat until slightly brown.
Add all ingredients to crock pot and cook on low 4-5 hours.
