Most Requested Healthy Cookies

Michelle K

Here it is, easily the most requested recipe I have!! Everyone who eats these asks for the recipe and then I chuckle a little and say these are so easy you’ll think I’m lying. But alas, I am not. There are so many great things about this recipe I love. There are 3 ingredients! They are ooey and super gooey, quick, easy and delicious healthier option cookies. This recipe does not make a ton so I am not tempted to eat 2 dozen cookies, and they’re perfect for a little family surprise dessert. I think my absolute favorite thing about these is how easy it is for the kids to help without me stressing about a mess.

I confess, I get stressed when those big brown eyes come and ask me if their little hands can “help” me in the kitchen. Every mom everywhere knows that just means you have more to clean up, but with these cookies it is so doable and dare I say even enjoyable for all. They are still processed chocolate chips so I can’t call them healthy, but they are a healthy and certainly healthier option than most. So, here it is the long awaited healthy option cookie recipe.

 

Healthy Option Cookies

Makes about 8-10 cookies

2 bananas

1 cup oats (GF oats for Gluten allergy)

½ cup chocolate chips (Enjoy Life-allergy friendly or dark chocolate)

Directions

Mash bananas add oats and chocolate chips. Mix. Roll 1-2 inch ball size “dough” with hands and place on cookie sheet. Bake at 350° for 10-12 minutes. Let cool a little but don’t miss out on the warm gooey, delicious mess they are fresh from the oven!

 

**Update as of 12/2019 this recipe with added 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract and 2 teaspoons of cinnamon won 1st place in a Holiday Cookie Contest!  I was shocked, there were about 100 participants!  These really are good!
