Quarantine Care Packages

Michelle K

Most of us are stuck at home and some people are going crazy!  It is a tough transition for a lot of people and I can't imagine having a work from home job and having younger elementary kids you're trying to help with this e-learning.  It is difficult and it is a great time for us as Christians to show love to our neighbors even if we can't be closer than 6 feet away.  

Here is an idea of what we did!  The options are limitless I am sure, so be creative in your own way and bless your neighbors!  We did a movie night quarantine care package!  You can go to Redbox.com and get a promo code gift card.  It may seem confusing, but I purchased the 5 promos gift card and had it sent to my own e-mail.  Then once it came to my e-mail I wrote the promo codes on the print outs to hand out to each neighboy.

Once you get the promo codes I printed the Redbox move night printable from here.  I liked these because a lot of them were teacher specific ones, but these were more generic so I could use them for neighbors.  We got .50 gift bags, gave them a few hundred grand (the candy bar) for hard times, microwave popcorn and wrote a note of encouragement.  Normally we would do this anonymously but this time we wanted to put our names and phone numbers if our neighbors needed anything to let us know.  We delivered them to neighbors by placing them, oh so stealthily on their front porch.  Great way for kids to serve!  This is the time for the church to rise up, to serve, to love others and to be the hands and feet of Christ even if we can't do that face to face.  I hope this inspires you to serve your neighbors, unebelievers or friends and family in some way during this time also!
