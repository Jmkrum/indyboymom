Free/inexpensive educational resources

Michelle K

With all that is going on there are some amazing free trials and free subscriptions going on too!  Business are getting creative and also just wanting to meet a great need that is out there currently.  Here is a list of 4 education resources that I highly recommend!  Some of them are free for a limited time only.

  1. IAHE.net
    • Indiana Homeschool Convention is online for free until April 10th.  After April 10th I believe there will be a small fee, but this is an amazing resource.  Not only are there solid teachings and workshops to equip and encourage home education there is also an online exhibit hall to check out curriculum, books, educational games and the list goes on!  I attend the Indiana Homeschool Convention each year and have never been disappointed.
  2. Teach Them Diligently
    • I have not been to a Teach Them Diligently conference yet, but I am extremely excited that they are offering so much of their conference online right now.  It is $20 (super cheap for so much GREAT info) or $60 which includes a membership (membership is normally $99, so another great deal).  I really respect the leadership at Teach Them Diligently and this is another great resource for looking into homeschooling or for veteran homeschoolers.
  3. Parent Summit
    • This is a parent summit, lot's of solid speakers encouraging parents.  We have attended their online homeschool summits before and enjoyed the Biblical teaching we received so I am sure this new parenting summit will be uplifting and Biblical also.  It is free.
  4. Financial Peace University
    • We love Dave Ramsey and I respect that during this difficult time financially for so many people Ramsey Solutions is offering the Financial Peace University class absolutely free for 2 weeks.  You can totally binge watch it.  Even if you have already seen it, chances are it could be an older version you saw and you will learn something new or hear something afresh in it.  This is a GREAT tool to pass on to friends or family as we all know people drastically impacted financially through these recent times.
