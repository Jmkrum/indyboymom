Cooking with kids

Michelle K

During this time being home more often than usual I have been enjoying each night a different boy cooking by my side.  It's been a lot of fun and a great bonding time together.  I stumbled upon this website kidscookrealfood.com and I absolutely love her process!  She does a great job of meeting a child where they're at and helping teach the parents/guardians how to let the child help in the kitchen.  I wanted to highlight this because right now she is offering a 2 week free trial and after that for a limited time you can get a 2 month membership for just $19.  This is a great deal, and you don't have to decide until your two week free trial is up anyway.  

So, enjoy the time with your kids!  Bake and cook in the kitchen together.  Teach them skills (or maybe learn them yourself) that you normally wouldn't take the time to learn together.  Now is your chance and I highly recommend this course to help you!
