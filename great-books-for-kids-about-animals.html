Great books for kids about animals

Michelle K

I don't know if I have met a kid who does not like animals.  Perhaps some animals, like a snake or spiders, but overall most kids love most animals.  If your child is struggling to be engaged in reading a great way to combat that is finding books they would be engaged in, like books about animals.  This is a list of books or book series that we have thoroughly enjoyed.  Some are funny, some are serious, but every single one has captivated my boys.  Most of them I read to them, so whether you are reading to your kids or they are old enough to read them, these main character animal books are sure to engage your child!

 

Paddington by Michael Bond- Chances are you have already heard of Paddington, it is a classic.  This bear is so loveable and his inadvertent mischeif will keep your whole family smiling and laughing the entire book.  My oldest son loved Paddington so much for a few weeks he was found wearing a blue coat and begging to put a marmalade sandwich in his red hat in case of an emergency.  The first movie is quite cute also and fun to watch after reading the book.

Dog Chronicles by Alison Hart-My oldest came upon these books at the library and we have fallen in love with them!  If you want to engage your kids in history while following the heroism of a dog they will fall in love with, these are the books for you.  We were all glued to every page.  If your kids are 6 and under it may be harder to hold their attention as there are not many pictures, but even my 5 year old boy loved these books.  They follow a specific dog into an action packed story that will have you and your kids unable to put the book down.  There are 4 different titles we have found:

  • Finder: Coal Mine Dog
  • Leo: Dog of the Sea
  • Darling: Mercy Dog of World War I
  • Murphy: Gold Rush Dog

Mercy Watson by Kate DiCamillo: You may or may not be familiar with Mercy Watson a pet pig who loves toast with a great deal of butter on it, but you are probably familiar with this wonderful, engaging author: Kate DiCamillo.  She is probably most famous for Because of Winn Dixie or The Tale of Despereaux both later made into great movies.  Mercy Watson books are illustrated by Chris Van Dusen an excellent illustrator with pictures that keep kids of all ages engaged and intrigued in the story.  

Henry Huggins by Beverly Cleary-A Newberry Medal Award winning author writes these hilarous books about a boy and his new dog Ribsy.  You and your kids will love these fun tales with Henry and Ribsy and the mischief they find in their daily adventures as best friends.

Marvels of Creation by Buddy Davis-If it's fiction your kids are more interested in these books will hit the spot.  The photos are gorgeous and there is a page dedicated to each animal so the information load is perfect for a younger or older reader. There are 3 books in this series featuring animals from different areas.  These are also from a Biblical worldview and creationist view which is a breath of fresh air for us!

  • Sensational Sea Creatures
  • Magnificent Mammals
  • Breathtaking Birds
