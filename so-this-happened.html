So, This Happened

Michelle K

That tooth was not loose.  Sam and his brother were running around and his older brother tugged on his arm as he was running through the doorway, however his tug pulled Sam's face a little too close to the door frame.  Face meet wooden door frame.  There was blood immediately and a look of terror...thankfully...on the older brothers face realizing he probably shouldn't tug his brother through the doorway while running.

The baby started crying because Sam was crying and Sam was crying because all he saw was blood coming out of his mouth.  All I could see was white and blood while a baby clawed at me to pick him up.  I got Samuel a wet rag and once the blood slowed I realized the white was a dangling tooth.  Apparently he could tell by the look on my face I was stunned so he began crying again but the rag hit his tooth and plop out came a tooth.  His response, no more tears but a shout of delight as he held up the valuable item and yelled, "I am rich!!!"  I didn't even know he knew about the tooth fairy.  The older brother asked if the tooth fairy still comes when I tooth wasn't loose in the first place and I said I would imagine you get more money taking this route.

So, we have a toothless 5 year old and probably a toothless 6 year old and 7 and possibly even 8 and 9 year old.  Here's to great family pictures the next few years!  Life with 3 boys is never dull.
